About Me

I’m a website developer based in Bristol, UK. I have a passion for building things with HTML5, CSS3(Sass), JavaScript, React.js and Node.js. I love the challenge of creating, and I love figuring out how stuff works. In the initial phase of every project, I plan ruthlessly, making prototypes in Balsamiq mock ups or Photoshop. My CMS of choice is Wordpress...


My visual background began a long time ago when I studied photography and media production at college. I then achieved a degree in cinematography at The Arts University College, Bournemouth, which deepened my appreciation of making a strong initial plan and from there developing a piece of work to its maximum potential.


After some time in the film industry, I decided to travel and have since worked in the mountains of France and the beaches of Italy. After deciding to come back to England, I moved to Bristol and worked as a chef whilst I built up my portfolio and clients. Besides building web sites I have a passion for surfing, mountain biking, snowboarding, films and music.
